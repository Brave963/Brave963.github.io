Ownership

Private Enterprise (Employee-Owned)

Huawei is not a listed company (Public Limited Company). It is 100% owned by its employees through a Union.

Advantage: Avoids short-term pressure from stock market investors, allowing massive long-term R&D spending.

COMPETITION STRATEGY09

Demand & Supply Scenario

Technical Progress & Economies of Scale

As Huawei implements highly automated production and achieves larger scales of operation, the Marginal Cost (MC) of production decreases.

Economic Effect: Supply of equipment increases.

ANALYSIS SCENARIO10

DSE Analysis

"When production scale increases, Supply shifts from S1 to S2."

1. Price: Falls from P1 to P2.

2. Quantity: Rises from Q1 to Q2.

DSE ECONOMIC DIAGRAM11

Explanation & Conclusion

As Supply increases, a shortage occurs at the original price P1.

This puts downward pressure on price. As price falls, quantity demanded increases (Law of Demand) while quantity supplied decreases (Law of Supply).

Final Result: Lower Equilibrium Price & Higher Equilibrium Quantity.
